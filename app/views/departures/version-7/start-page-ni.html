Submit union transport declarations through NCTS

Log in to the New Computerised Transit System (NCTS) to submit union transit declarations to HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC).

 

From: HM Revenue & Customs

Published: 18 July 2021

Applies to: Wales, England, and Scotland (see guidance for Northern Ireland)
